about

role = "ML Systems Engineer" // kernels, inference, infra

I work underneath the models — writing the kernels, schedulers, and serving layers that decide whether a forward pass takes 40ms or 400ms. Most of my time goes into inference engines, GPU memory management, and distributed execution: the kind of problems that show up when you try to serve a model at scale instead of just running it once in a notebook.

Think along the lines of what vLLM does — continuous batching, paged attention, kernel fusion, tensor/pipeline parallelism — that's the layer I live in. I like systems that are measured in milliseconds and megabytes, not epochs.
